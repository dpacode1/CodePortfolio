Summary

The Black House is an eccentric but cozy cafe within Indianapolis. The coffeeshop is themed after all things horror, as decorations from iconic movies such as “Nightmare on Elm Street,” and “The Gremlins,” line the walls. The Black House truly shows that a themed entertainment venue doesn’t need to have a lot of complicated technology in order to be fun and enjoyable. This video acts as a study for a small establishment that provides a themed experience while providing a service.
