Summary

This project was intended to help students find their own style when it comes to branding themselves. The class in which this project was designed to help students learn how to promote themselves, and to close the gap between an academic career and a professional career. During my concepting phase, I focused heavily on my skills. I found that my ability to work in different types of media was a strength, so I heavily inspired my brand in space to show those ‘out of this world’ results.
