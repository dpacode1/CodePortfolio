Summary

This project was intended to be a study on social media posts, and how to quickly provide information to a large group of people in a visually interesting way. I found that large images, and vibrant colors appeared to be the most successful when it comes to the limited amount of space that certain social media sites (such as Instagram) allow for images. By the end of this study, I realize that boldness is the way to go in a world where attention spans are short and information needs to be provided quickly.
