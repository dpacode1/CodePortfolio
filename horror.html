Summary

I have always found the horror medium to be interesting, as it appears to be an underutilized genre when it comes to immersive storytelling. This project was intended to focus on different horror creators and study both their story techniques and their artistic style. This project was self-indulgent, and I have found a new love for the horror genre as a whole by the end of the creation of the presentation.
